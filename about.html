Biography

I am a freelance writer who loves writing other people’s stories. I find them fascinating and have written numerous magazine articles of that nature. Currently, I'm working on a memoir.

For more than twenty-five years, I ministered alongside my husband Derek—including pioneering two churches, heading up women’s ministries as well as founding a prayer ministry for mothers of prodigals.

My sense of adventure and insatiable love for travel has taken me to Spain, Israel, Egypt, Switzerland, Amsterdam, Kenya as well as numerous visits to South Africa, my husband’s homeland. Because I believe it’s a crime to go to Africa without going on Safari, this has often been an added adventure. One day, I hope to visit the Serengeti to experience the Great Migration.

I am a voracious reader and enjoy decorating, knitting, and hosting tea parties. I cherish quality time with family and friends—especially sharing tea with my great-granddaughters.
