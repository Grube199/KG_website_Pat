Who We Are

We are a group of Christians who meet together and pray specifically for the return of prodigals.

Vision

Patricia van der Merwe founded the Deborah Ministries when one of her children became a prodigal. Her desire is to reach others experiencing this same heart ache and to give them hope. She's written and published numerous articles on the subject of prayer.

Purpose

To teach and use Biblical prayer strategies.

Bible Verse

The Lord gave the word: great was the company of those that published it. Psalm 68:11, KJV.
